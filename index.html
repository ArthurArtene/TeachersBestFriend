Help & Tips

Seating Chart Generator

  • Adding Items: Use the buttons in the control panel to add desks and room components to the canvas.
  • Moving Items: Click and drag any item to move it around the canvas. It will automatically snap to the grid.
  • Item Controls: Hover over any item to reveal its controls:
    • Magnet Icon: Snaps the item perfectly to the nearest grid line.
    • X Icon: Deletes the item from the canvas.
    • Rotate Handle: Click and drag the handle below the item to rotate it in 15-degree increments.
  • Saving/Loading: Name your layout, click "Save", and it will appear in the dropdown. You can load or delete saved layouts at any time. Your layouts are saved in your browser.
  • Importing Names: Click "Upload CSV" to quickly import a list of names from a CSV file. The file should contain names separated by commas or new lines.
  • Harkness Table: Select a number of seats and click "Add Harkness Table" to add a large, collaborative table to your layout.

Random Group Generator

  • Enter Names: Type or paste names into the text area, one name per line. You can also use the "Upload CSV" button.
  • Set Group Count: Enter the total number of groups you want to create.
  • Generate: Click "Generate Groups" to randomly and evenly distribute the names into the specified number of groups.

Classroom Timer

  • Set a Timer: Enter the number of minutes and click "Set".
  • Display Mode: After setting a timer, choose your preferred view: Analog, Digital, or Both.
  • Controls: Use the "Start", "Pause", and "Reset" buttons to control the countdown.
  • Focus Music: Paste a YouTube video URL and click "Load". After loading, you can use the "Hide/Show Video" button to toggle the video display while keeping the audio.
